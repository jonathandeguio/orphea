Android application development process

Project Analysis

The first step of our Android Application Development lifecycle is to analyze the project requirements, whenever necessary, refine them, and establishing a direction after each step. This step also involves the team at MoveToData to understand the data requirements of the project and identifying the necessary data structures and databases to be developed.

Data Architecture and UI/UX Design

Once we have the requirements, we develop a strong data model and information architecture that controls all the flow of data within the software and ensure there are no loose ends. Further, we aim to cater the perfect experience to users operating our Android Application. Thus, we invest time in User research and develop a UI/UX that is easily accessible.

Development

The next step is carrying out the development of the project in accordance with global coding practices. Our team ensures standard development procedures to achieve an innovative solution that not just meets but exceeds client expectations.

Quality Assurance and Maintenance

Quality Assurance is a commitment for everything we do at MoveToData. Java-based applications developed by MoveToData undergo rigorous testing and quality check procedures to ensure ironing out bugs.
