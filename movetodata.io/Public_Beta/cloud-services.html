Overview

Right from its inception, MoveToData Solutions has been a strong proponent of adopting the latest technologies in order to develop a strategic competitive edge as well as leveraging the potential of new-age technology platforms to optimize business efficiency. In recent times, one of the most sought-after technologies is cloud services.

Whether it is building customized applications for cloud or migrating your existing data to cloud-based storage, there is a wide range of opportunities organizations can utilize to improve their digital infrastructure. MoveToData Solutions offers its customers with a variety of Cloud Consulting and Development Services right from consultancy to the deployment of a customized solution that is tailor-made in accordance with an organization’s requirements. Our team of technical experts is equipped with the most advanced knowledge about the best cloud computing practices and technologies like Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, etc.

Cloud Consulting Expertise

The prerequisite to implementing a cloud-based system in your organization is to identify the right platform and technology that will suit your requirements the best. With an in-depth knowledge of different market leaders like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and GCP, you can count upon MoveToData’s discretion when it comes to finding the right Cloud Partner for your project. .

Cloud Application Development

MoveToData houses a talented pool of cloud application developers and business analysts help you build a customized Cloud Application that is robust, secure and functional to achieve your goals.

Cloud Migration

With MoveToData, Customers can also migrate their existing database infrastructure to more sophisticated and secure cloud-based databases. We also provide our customers with SaaS enabling services, making sure all of their requirements are taken care of under the same roof.

Cloud Architecture

One of the most important aspects of any successful application is its database architecture. We, at MoveToData, understand the intricacies associated with taking your business on the cloud. Our database experts build an architecture that is consistent, durable and credible such that all the information stored is secure.

Microsoft Azure Cloud Services

Developed by Microsoft, Azure is a public cloud-based service that is operated through 34 powerful Data Centers across the world, enabling organizations to run their apps anywhere. The technology offers a wide range of aspects that help take businesses to the next level through acute insights and optimized operations. These services include analytics, computing, database, mobile, networking, storage, and Web.

Amazon Web Services (AWS) Cloud Services

MoveToData has expertise to architect, deliver, and manage an AWS cloud platform to provide services such as DevOps Pipeline for Serverless Application, CI/CD (Continuous Integration & Continuous Deployment), AWS EC2, S3 bucket, Auto Scaling Group, Cloud template formation, Elasticbeanstalk, AWS Lambda, Kubernetes, Docker, Jenkins, Route 53, DynamoDB etc.

Software Development Lifecycle

Project Analysis

The first step involves identifying in detail the requirements of any project and understanding the functionality that each and every user might require. Upon analyzing requirements, we also help our customers to refine them and then the right technology platforms that would best meet the user requirements.

Designing the User Interface

One of the most important features of any software is to have a User Interface and Experience that is easy to navigate for every category of users. At MoveToData, we design a User Interface that is functional, user-friendly, and visually appealing.

Development

Once the requirements are frozen and user interface design finalized, we proceed to development work for the software while inculcating the best coding practices such that the end result is robust, secure and efficient software.

Quality Assurance and Maintenance

Quality Assurance is a commitment for everything we do at MoveToData. We deploy any Software Product Development project after carrying out comprehensive testing. Our Quality Control department ensures there are no bugs before it is rolled out. Upon successful deployment of the software, we also help with version controls and maintenance of the software.
