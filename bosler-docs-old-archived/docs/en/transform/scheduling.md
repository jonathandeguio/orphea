# Scheduling

<div style="text-align: justify"> A schedule is a recurring build task that ensures a steady flow of data in Bosler. The trigger in a schedule determines when the build should run.

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">A schedule is considered executed when the trigger conditions are met, and the build is performed. If a schedule is triggered while another is still running, it will remain on hold until the current schedule is completed before being executed.</p>

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">Scheduling and Builds are linked together. You can set triggers to schedules builds of datasets in Bosler</p>

## Creating a schedule

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">In any datasets, there is a schedule option below.</p>

![Schedule1](../../_media/schedule1.1.png)

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">Selecting the Schedule option, you can choose exactly when you want to trigger this build consistently automatically.</p>

### Customising Triggers

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">You are able to customise the schedule trigger down to the second giving you all options to ensure you can use Bosler to your liking.</p>

![Schedule2](../../_media/schedule2.png)

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">You can customize either by time or by source.</p>

![Schedule3](../../_media/schedule3.png)

<p style="text-indent: 20px;">To check your schedules table, click on the schedules on the sidebar menu.</p></div>
