What is our platform?

BOSLER is a modern data platform, it is a collection of tools and capabilities that, when brought together, allow organizations to achieve the gold standard — a fundamentally data-driven organization.

Given the sheer scale and complexity of data today, it’s no longer enough for a modern data platform to process and store data.

DATA PLATFORM LIKE NO OTHER.

We Are A Data Platform Provider Based In Toulouse, France. We Put Our Customer's Needs At The Forefront Of Our Product Design, Putting Our Customers Firmly In The Driving Seat So That They Take Control While We Ensure That The Data Platform Always Delivers To Their Needs.

AI & MACHINE LEARNING

We provide the platform with the capabilities to create algorithms for AI and Machine learning, with years of experience to guide new teams. We support python, pyspark, R and other languages.

GREAT SUPPORT

We help you set up the platform on premise, or with the cloud provider of your choice. Once set up, we will be always there to support you and guide through tasks such as ingesting data, creating data pipelines, and visualization.

FULLY RESPONSIVE

The Data Platform's visualization content is not only real-time and live, but it can also be used on all modern devices. We not only provide in-built visualization tools, you can also plug-in your own in-house tools such as TIBCO Spotfire, Qlik etc.

Why you Choose Bosler

Made in Europe We have designed and developed the data platform within Europe. This is also for the security-conscious who want their data to be on a secure platform.

Dataset VersioningA key feature of our platform is data versioning, just like git! It means that multiple team members can work on same pipelines, but also, they can roll back to previous versions of the data and pipelines. We support python, pyspark, R out of the box, and we provide templates for you to get started quickly.

Real TimeBosler not only analyzes cold data but we can also visualise in real-time

SpeedBosler is not just fast, it is superfast!! We have a design philosophy that, if a customer has to wait more than 4 seconds for a page to load, then re-think how it works. We will not include bloated code; we go down to binary level (the zeros and ones) to improve the data platform's performance.

Security By Design All of the Bosler data platform's components follow the same rules for security. All components use token based security. However, we can also authenticate with most modern authorization protocols (OAuth2, OpenID) and legacy. We help you set up the platform on premise, or the cloud provider of your choice. Once it is set up, we will be there to support you and guide through ingesting data, creating data pipelines, and visualization.

Customer SupportThis is at the heart of Bosler. We put the customer first in everything. Our philosophy is to help the customer grow.
