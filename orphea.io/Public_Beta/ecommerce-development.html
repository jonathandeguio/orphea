Move beyond brick and motor with Orphea’s

eCommerce has revolutionised the way the world shops. While earlier, retail was a completely offline market, brands like Amazon and Alibaba have altered consumer behaviour beyond recognition. People have moved on from buying books to more expensive goods like phones and personal computers online. It is imperative for retail businesses to leverage this shift in consumer behaviour to attract customers from their traditional locations.

Orphea Solutions, a leading eCommerce Developer, provides customers with state-of-the-art solutions that utilize the power of technology to attract new customers for your business and generate sustainable revenue from a completely new channel. We, at Orphea, are well-versed with popular eCommerce Content Management Systems like WooCommerce, Shopify, and Magento as well as technologies like React and Node that are most popular to build customized eCommerce portals from the scratch.

Advantages of eCommerce

  • Reaching out to new customers, there is no theoretical geographic limitations
  • Customers can easily select products from various vendors without moving physically
  • Social media marketing like Facebook, Instagram and digital marketing like Google Adwords, Bing are cost effective ways to market your online business
  • Customer Data Insights: To collect, measure, and act on customer data to edge ahead from your competitors
  • eCommerce website or Mobile application will help you to serve niche markets
  • eCommerce allows sellers to remove friction points as customers can complete purchases from Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, Data Transformation in-store and more
  • Being able to showcase best sellers and products to customers
  • Affordable employees: Fewer employees require in an online eCommerce business than a retail location
