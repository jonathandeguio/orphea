Frontend Application Development Technology

As websites and web-based applications continue to gain prominence for avenues like e-learning, eCommerce, organizational applications, SaaS-based products, organizations are investing heavily in enhancing the customer experience in order to retain their loyalty in a highly competitive market. Orphea houses an expert team of designers and developers that curate a simple, functional, and visually appealing front-end for your Websites or Web-based Application.

There is a wide range of new technologies that have come up in the past few years that go beyond the traditional HTML-CSS-JavaScript framework. These include React, React Native, AngularJS, and Vue.JS. While React was developed Facebook, it was Google that invented AngularJS. Orphea houses developers with expertise in both of them who develop solutions using either, depending on the requirements of our customers.

Software Development Lifecycle

Requirement Analysis and Gathering

We, at Orphea Solutions, obtain all the necessary requirements from our customers and often guide them with suggestions that can help their application better. Once we have the requirements, we develop a roadmap to achieve our desired results. Further, we also help clients choose from the rich variety of technologies available for front end development.

Wireframe Development

For any UI/UX development project, it is important for developers to understand how information and data flow through the application as well as how to serve every different user class. Thus, before moving on to development, our designers interact with different users and understand their concerns and then develop wireframes.

Development

Equipped with the best coding practices and standardized tools, we at Orphea Solutions seamlessly carry out the perfect Front-end Design and Development Services.

Quality Assurance and Maintenance

At Orphea Solutions, we have a stringent quality policy that dictates enforcement of quality standards on every aspect of solutions we provide. For User Interface design projects, not only does our team comprehensively test but we also ask some actual users to test the application.

Business Models

Collaborate with a leading team of developers as well as business analysts that realize your ideas of a customized Web Application. Our designers and developers for front end development projects can contribute through many different business models and are flexible in accordance with our client’s requirements.

Hiring Dedicated developer

This model is ideal for organizations seeking a long-term development team that can see through their entire project. Our team is capable of working from your premises as well as operating as an offshore front end development agency.

Hour Based Hiring

We recommend this model to organizations that are completely aware of the requirements as well as have a rigid timeline to follow.

Time and Material Model

Under this model, customers compensate in accordance with the time and other tangible resources that we utilize while executing the project.
